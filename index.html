Background

Rural People Development Initiative is an independent, non-religious, non-political and non-governmental organization that is out for the development of the poorest of the poor in Oke-Ogun and Nigeria at large. RUPEDIN was founded in 1997. It was Rural Search Light Nigeria(RUSEL) but latter became Rural People Development Initiative during the CAC registration.

Since her inception RUPEDIN affected many life positively thus making life better by improving the quality of life better by improving the quality of life the target groups. Over 2000 people enrolled and enjoying the services of RUPEDIN.

RUPEDIN has received a resolving loan assistance from Community Development Foundation (CDI) Lagos, and lift Above Poverty (LAPO) Benin City. RUPEDIN is one of the beneficiaries of DFID/CBDD Genetic Training Programmes as well as Organizational Assessment and Development (OA/OD). IRED Anglophone West Africa, Lagos also trained RUPEDIN staff and members Aug 2006, RUPEDIN was one of the beneficiaries of ASA methodology in micro-credit. We also participated in the Micro-Credit Summit Campaign on Integration of Health Education, HIV/AIDS into Micro Finance.

Over three hundred groups have benefitted from our monthly and weekly credit facilities while youth were trained to be computer literate and men women were empowered with motorcycles, grinding engine and provision of fertilizers to our farmer memebers.

Mission

To create an enabling environment that will allow members, the less priviledged of the women / youths and the poorest of the poor in Oke-Ogun and Nigeria, to achieve healthy and improved living standard and sustain their developmental objectives.

Corporate Status

Rural People Development Initiative registered with Kajola Local Government in 1997. Ministry of Women Affairs and Social Development in 1998. In the year 2006 registered with Corporate Affairs Commission CAC Abuja.

Objectives

  • To mobilize and encourage rural and semi-urban people especially women and youths imbibing the spirit of self help as a model of the community development
  • To support the development of all semi-scale rural based technology needful for sustenance of community based enterprices including information and communication technology.
  • To mobilize funds towards further wealth creation in the community.
  • To initiate, promote and execute programs that will encourage and enhance the culture, social and economic development in the community.
  • To promote neat environment, good health and sound education in the community.
  • To aid farmers, traders, and small-scale food processors in having opportunities to make bulk purchases.
  • To offers training and business development services to micro and small enterprices owners.
  • To co-operate with other organizations with similar objectives.
  • To undertake such other activities that may reasonably incidental to aims and objectives herein.

Target Groups

Rural People Development Initiative (RUPEDIN) work and assist the bottom poor, such as;

  • Unorganized and conducted rural and semi-urban based people.
  • Existing women and youth cooperative organization.
  • Community development groups.
  • Community development assocations.
  • People living with HIV/AIDs
